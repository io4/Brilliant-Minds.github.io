Rules

  1. No trolling of any kind
  2. No spamming
  3. No abusing of group manager privileges
  4. No inappropriate/hateful/racist posts
  5. Don't get your TPT account banned without an adequate reason
  6. Don't lie about anything serious to Higger rank famil member

Contribution Rules

Anyone may make a contribution to the group. It will serve to further your place in the familly, as well as get you a bit more well known. Any and all contributions made to the familly become our property. i.e. if you make a new logo, and then get perma-banned from the group, that logo belongs to the familly, and we may continue to use it.

Information Leakage

Leaking BMN information can be a serious offence. All members are supposed to be responsible and mature enough to not tell non-familly members information held within the familly. Especially to Banned Members. Giving information to Banned members will lead to discharge and perma-banning.

Post Rules

Anyone may make a post while respecting these rules. If you don't follow these rules once you will be warned, the next time you will get a probation.

  • You may not use more than five big (<big></big>) html tags.
  • You may not make posts that are really long.
  • You may not double post using an alternative account in a post contest.
  • You may not include giant images (bigger than 256px), only post a link to the image.

For any other few-days ban, the Don will ask you for the reason and then ask jacob1 about it. If the reasons don't match, you will be demoted once.
For any unnecessary ban, a trial and a hearing will be convened. This will determine what punishment is given, if any.
