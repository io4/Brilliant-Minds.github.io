Goals

I wish to make it the goal of all of BMN to be the greatest at what we do. My goal is to have at least two BMN member-made saves on the first page at the same time, and that whenever someone searches the words Ship, Sea, Weapon or Bomb, they see BMN member-made saves first.

Rules and Trials

  • No trolling of any kind
  • No spamming
  • No abusing of group manager privilegues
  • No inappropiate/hateful/racist posts
  • Don't get you TPT account banned without an adequate reason
  • Don't lie about anything serious or to a Commanding Officer
Probation Punishments

For anything excessively profane, if you got banned for it in TPT, you will be demoted and placed on level 3 probation for 4 weeks, then you will be moved to level 1 probation.
If you get 5 punishments like this, you will be banned from the group.

For any other few-days ban, the Fleet Admiral will ask you for the reason and then ask jacob1 about it. If the reasons don't match, you will be demoted once.
For any unnescessary ban, a trial and a hearing will be convened. This will determine what punishment is given, if any.

Probation Levels
  • Level 1: Light restrictions from group activities, it will not affect your promotion. May go on your record and lasts for one week.
  • Level 2: Medium restrictions from group activities. May affect your promotion during the next Inactive Movement Cycle, and this may go on your record.
    If you receive this and then receive a level 3 probation, you will be discharged. Lasts for 3 weeks.
  • Level 3: Whatever you did to deserve this, it was bad. Heavy restrictions from group activities, no promotion in the next two IMCs, and if you get another level 2 or higher probation, you will be discharged.
    Also, any Admirals Counsel members who are a group manager and get this probation will be changed down to a Member for 2 months. Will go on you record. Lasts for 3 months.
  • Level 4 (waiting list): This essentially means that we are deciding who you are being replaced with. Goodbye.
Trials
For any severe (or at least partially) offense made by an Admiral, a trial will be convened. There shall be a prosecutor, defense attourney, jury of peers (people of the same counsel), testimonials and a judge (the current Fleet Admiral). All people participating in this trial will have their ranks suspended for reasons against biased judgement, except the judge.
Any actual offense made by an Admiral is more serious than that of any other member, obviously. But if an Admiral is being prosecuted, no one below the AC sit-in Commander may post to the thread. Breaking this rule is punishable by discharge.

    Admirals Counsel

    The Admirals Counsel is comprised of the Admiral positions. The AC is where all rule creations, changes and fixes will be made, as well as promotions, demotions and member removals (discharges).
    • For a member to be removed, three out of the five top Admirals have to vote yes.
    • For a demotion, any four Admirals must vote for yes, unless vetoed by an Admiral or the current Fleet Admiral.
    • Promotions need four Admirals to vote for yes, of course the new rank must be open and not taken yet.
    • A non-Admiral requires six Admirals to vote yes so he/she can join the AC.
    • For a rule creation or change, more than half of the Admirals need to vote yes. For a rule fix, it only needs three unless vetoed by the top five Admirals.
    Any thread that is tagged with "(AC)" means that it is an Admirals Counsel discussion only. You may watch it, but only Captains and Commander AC sit-ins max post there.
    There may be a few exceptions where we allow Captains, Commanders and maybe even Lieutenant Commanders into the AC.

    Rates and Ranks of BMN

    Officer Ranks
    • Fleet Admiral Highest power of BMN. Head of the Admirals Counsel. There is only one of these at a time.
    • Admiral Second highest power. High leadership position in the AC. There are only two of these, and they can veto any decision made by the Fleet Admiral.
    • Vice Admiral AC leadership position. There are only two of these, and them, along with the rest of the lower Admirals, can veto any decision by the Fleet Admiral and/or the Admirals.
    • Rear Admiral (Upper Half) Leadership position, member of the AC. There are only up to two of these at a time. Can veto with Vice Admiral and Rear Admiral LH.
    • Rear Admiral (Lower Half) Leadership position, low member of the AC. There are only two of them at a time, and they usually do the "paperwork" (or at least what counts as paperwork in TPT) of the AC. Can veto with Vice Admiral and Rear Admiral UH.
    • Captain There are only two of these, and they are the leaders of the Medium Officers Counsel. One Captain will be a sit-in member of the AC and can put his/her own views in.
    • Commander There are only three of these, and they are the Second-in-Commands of the MOC. One of them will also be a member of the AC and can put his/her own views in.
    • Lieutenant Commander There are only three of these, and they are in the last Officer position to also have a place in the AC. He/she can only put in views if approved by both the AC Captain and the AC Commander. This is also the last member position of the MOC.
    • Lieutenant There are three of these, and one of them is the leader of the Lower Officers Counsel. This leader is also a sit-in member of the MOC and can put his/her views into play.
    • Lieutenant, Junior Grade There are four of these, and they are the Second-in-Command position of the LOC. They are also the second-to-last Officer rank.
    • Ensign There are five of these, and they are the lowest Officer ranks, as well as the lowest member of the LOC.
    Preofficer Ranks
    • Officer Candidate Essentially the waiting list to replace Officers which are being removed.
    • Upper Recruit One below Officer Candidate. They begin training for Officer ranks here.
    • Lower Recruit Everyone starts here. As long as you post 5 times, you will be promoted. If you don't get promoted from here within a week, you will be removed.
